ABOUT US

An Electromechanical Contracting company focusing mainly in the Design, Supply and Installation of Automatic Firefighting Suppression Systems, Fire Detection Alarm Systems and Maintenance Services, located at 325 Narvaez St., San Agustin 1, Dasmariñas City, Cavite. Since inception, ALGON has steadfastly become one of the major players in the industry within CALABARZON Region.

Previously, it is a sole proprietorship company formerly known ALGON ENTERPRISES, which was established in April 04, 2001 under the ownership and management of MR. EDUARDO L. ALVARAN and originally, a technical support of a fire protection supplier in Cavite and served several general contractors in their Automatic Fire Suppression Requirements of its projects. Due to the increasing demand of our services/supplies to businesses, the company came up with a very timely and ambitious decision, this is to extricate from being just a technical support and be recognized in services/supplies provision identified in the corporate industry.

Composed with the same qualified and competitive technical team, still headed by MR. EDUARDO L. ALVARAN - President/Chief Operating Officer, the rebirth of ALGON FIRETECH CORPORATION took the spot in the industry. The company went several stages and procedures which finally led to be recognized by Security and Exchange Commission last June 11, 2019.

As ALGON FIRETECH CORPORATION emerged in the business industry, it was the only company in the Philippines granted with an Exclusive Distributorship of TNA FIRE ALARM SYSTEMS manufactured by TANDA TECHNOLOGIES PTE. LTD. SINGAPORE with head office at TANDA UK LTD. United Kingdom. These products are U/L (Underwriters Laboratories) and EN-54-2 and EN-54-4 (European Standards) Listed and are LPCB (Loss Prevention Certification Board) Certified.

Fire Detection and Alarm System

For a business, a working, properly maintained fire alarm system is essential to the safety of employees, customers, and visitors. It provides a quick-response method to alert the building’s occupants to an emergency, enabling a timely, safe evacuation. To keep your business safe and up to code, your fire alarm systems must be inspected and maintained on a regular schedule.

Automatic Sprinkler System

Fire sprinkler systems are your business’s first line of defense against a fire. Though they may not stop all the damage, according to the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA), buildings with a sprinkler system often suffer 60% less damage than buildings that lack them. Considering the devastation that can occur in a fire which can be a significant amount of savings during the rebuilding process.

FIRE PUMP / JOCKEY PUMP

A properly maintained fire pump is essential to the safety of a commercial building, providing the water necessary for optimal sprinkler operation. When a fire pump is working correctly, it helps to avoid injuries and fatalities during a fire while significantly reducing the damage at your facility. Regular inspection and maintenance are required to ensure a fire pump will operate properly during an emergency, our experts have the skills and equipment to keep your fire pumps and sprinkler systems in NFPA-approved condition.
